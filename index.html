Frequently asked.

What services do you offer?

I offer a range of creative services including graphic design, video editing and production, French language tutoring, and strategic consulting. My unique background in physics allows me to approach each project with analytical precision while maintaining creative flair.

What languages do you speak?

I'm fluent in Spanish (native), French (C1), and English (C1). This trilingual ability allows me to work with clients from different cultures and create content that resonates across language barriers.

How does your process work?

My process follows four key steps: Listen (understanding your vision), Analyze (finding the best approach), Create (bringing ideas to life), and Refine (iterating until perfect). I believe in close collaboration and clear communication throughout every project.

What is AXKAN Souvenirs?

AXKAN Souvenirs is my business venture focused on being "Detonadores de Orgullo Mexicano" (Triggers of Mexican Pride). We create products and experiences that celebrate Mexican culture and identity, combining design excellence with cultural significance.

Do you offer French lessons?

Yes! I offer personalized French lessons for all levels. My teaching approach goes beyond grammar and vocabulary—I help students understand the French way of thinking, making language learning a transformative experience.
